Letter 9 - Biggest uncertainty is future

No one knows what will happen tomorrow.

In reality, nothing is certain.
Today, we may be lovers but we have no right to say
we will be lover tomorrow.
I never believe those theory, will never do.

I once loved summer and hated rainy days.
Now I hate being in summer, love being in the rain.

There won't be no more sunshine in my world.
Having a cute sunshine for a short period was enough for my world...
