Letter 14 - Where the Rain Cries

It's rainy again ... in the middle of May.
Ohh May ... again ! When everything began and ended.

When we met for the first time, it was a sunny day.
It was in May, just like now.
Let's say it was sunny with blue sky the whole year.

It was my world where there was no dark cloud back then.

It hasn't stopped raining since you're gone.
There is no more blue sky or singing birds.
Just bunch of dark clouds and crows.
