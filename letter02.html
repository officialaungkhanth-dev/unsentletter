Letter 2 - A distant memory

Some moments linger, even as they fade away.

I don’t think about you the way I used to.
It’s quieter now.

There was a time when everything felt unfinished—
conversations paused mid-sentence,
words waiting for the right moment.

Now, those moments feel farther away. Not gone.
Just resting somewhere I don’t try to reach anymore.

I guess this is what distance looks like.
