Letter 11 - Scars on my heart

Maybe I was wrong, maybe I was unmatual.

I used to scare that you'd hate me.

As an overthinking person, it wasn't easy.

I once scared to lose you
And I lost you with the most stupid way possible.

They've been hunting me for all these years.
Now, they turned into scars which I can never heal.
