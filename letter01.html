Letter 1 - A quiet evening

A quick reflection. This letter holds a moment of unspoken feelings.

I wanted to tell you I’m doing okay.
Some days I really am.
Some days I just say it out loud and hope it becomes true.

I still catch myself thinking of you in small moments -
when the room goes quiet,
when something almost feels normal again.

I don’t know what I would say if I could send this.
So I’m leaving it here instead.
