Letter 4 - A place I used to stay

I am still here.

Even after, being alone once again. Still surviving in a small way.
Life keeps moving forward somehow.

Sometimes, I feel like these are ordinary days again,
Despite some midnight storms sometime keep me awake some nights.

I won't reach out to you, I can't since
I don't want to make the situation worse.
So I’m leaving this one here again.

每天都快乐.
