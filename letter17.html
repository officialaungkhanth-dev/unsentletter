Letter 17 - Something I finally understand

I think I understand things a little better now.
Not everything… but enough to stop asking those same questions.

There was a time when I needed answers.
Needed to know what I did wrong,
what I could fix,
what I could change to make you stay.

Now… I don’t look for those things as much.
I still think of you sometimes.
But it doesn’t feel as loud anymore.
