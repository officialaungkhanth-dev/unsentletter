Letter 10 - Bittersweet

I haven't sleep well since that day.
Or perhaps I should say I can't sleep.

They are not nightmares.
The thing is that those dreams never last long.
They still keep me awake all night somedays.

It's hard to believe I lost you because ....
It seemed like you were never mine.
Sounds bittersweet, isn't it ?

And I ended up loving those bittersweets.
