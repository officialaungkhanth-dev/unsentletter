Letter 8 - Those last summer nights

How do you spend holiday's Eve ?
Since that year-that day you left, there is no more events.

I couldn't find myself happy like I should be.

While we're together, everyday in my life could be everything
Chrismas ? New Year's ? Birthday ? You name it !

I neither have proofs nor reasons for
How much I love you or why do I love you.
What I do believe is that you might feel exactly like I do.
But I do hope, you don't end up like me.
