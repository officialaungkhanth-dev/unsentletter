Letter 23 - I’ll leave this here (again)

I don’t have much to say today.

Maybe that’s what it means to grow up
to stop turning feelings into noise.

I won’t write big promises.
I won’t beg the sky.

If you ever find it, you’ll know…
I didn’t hate you and I didn’t forget you.
I just learned to wait quietly.

每天都快乐，真的！
