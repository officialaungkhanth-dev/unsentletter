Letter 12 - The world is too small

We once shared same roof, same water.
I never imagined that we would be torn apart like today.

I have no strength to find you even if we're in the same city.

I never thought I would end up pointlessly.
All I wanted was to enjoy the moments together with you.
You used to be everything that
I wanted so bad that I forgot to care about myself.

Now .....! Everything was torn apart as we fell apart.
Even the world is too big, I wish I could find you again.
