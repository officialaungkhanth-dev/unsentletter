Letter 7 - Midnight storms

Life is long, you never know what will happen next !
It might be sunny today, but can't say there is no storm tomorrow.
If I knew the future, I would have fixed a long time ago.

You once told me to forget the past which I try not to listen.
Sometimes I blame myself for being stucked in the past.
But sometimes I just love staying with our memories.
I believe the past is the only place where my soul can have peace.

The future where you don't exist is way worse than
Every storms you can imagine.
