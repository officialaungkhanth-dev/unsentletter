Letter 15 - The day my world fell apart

Today marks two years since we parted.

How's life going ? I heard you were in relationship.
You must have been enjoying your life, I assume.
Together we once dreamed… now you without me.

For me, life hasn't changed at all.
My heart is still refuse to accept you won't be mine.
Yet my brain is also fighting against my heart.

No peace was found inside me since that day.
So, cigarettes and late night thoughts became my friends.
